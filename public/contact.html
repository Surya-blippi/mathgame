Frequently Asked Questions

Is Learn Fire really free?

Yes. The core game with all three modes (Math, English, and Quiz) is completely free to play in your browser. We support the game through optional advertising. There are no paywalls blocking educational content.

What devices does Learn Fire work on?

Learn Fire works on any device with a modern web browser that supports WebGL. This includes desktop computers (Windows, Mac, Linux), laptops, tablets, and smartphones (iOS and Android). No downloads or installations are needed.

Is it safe for my children?

Absolutely. Learn Fire contains no violent or inappropriate content. The "shooting" mechanic is directed at cartoon robots and is focused entirely on answering educational questions correctly. There are no social features, chat systems, or ways for strangers to contact your child through the game. We do not collect personal information from any users. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I suggest new questions or subjects?

We welcome content suggestions! If you are a teacher or subject matter expert and would like to contribute question sets, or if you have spotted an error in our existing questions, email us and we will review your submission.

The game is not loading on my device. What should I do?

First, ensure you are using an up-to-date browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). Try clearing your browser cache and reloading the page. If the issue persists, send us an email with your device model, browser name and version, and a description of what you see, and we will help troubleshoot.
